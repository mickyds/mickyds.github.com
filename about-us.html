Who we are

Mikayla J. Drust

The founder and CEO of Phoenix Arts. Mikayla lived in Blahblahblah, West Virginia until she recently moved to Austin, Texas. She always had an affinity for art, specifically cartoon wolves and dragons, but never knew what to do with her talent until 2019 when she started her own art company created this website.

Heather E. Armstrong

Heather is the co-CEO of Phoenix Arts and Mikayla's sister. While Mikayla has the skills as an artist, she's does not have the organizational skills that her sister has. Heather is the backbone of the company, keeping everything in check. She currently resides in Blahblahblah, West Virginia with her husband, Mike, and two children, Mikey and Lucy.

Little MickyD's

MickyD's is the mascot/spokesperson of Phoenix Arts. She wrote all the code of the website and manages the finances of the shop. She the face of the company.
